The Commute Game Plan: "Soletra" Edition

Every day, I take an Uber to work. My commute usually takes between 45 minutes and 1 hour and 15 minutes — it all depends on traffic.

To distract myself from the long commute, I start my day playing Soletra, a Brazilian version of Spelling Bee. The problem? I live in São Paulo, one of the Brazilian cities with the highest rates of cellphone theft.

Many of these crimes occur during traffic jams, when motorcyclists take advantage of the congestion to break car windows and steal from drivers stuck in traffic.
